How to use iConnect

1. Join the community

First, download the app and apply for membership. It’s a place to meet native speakers from all over the world.

2. Find a partner

Once approved, you can easily search for language partners based on your preferences like language, location, or interests.

3. Let’s have a conversation!

Chat via text, voice, or video. Built-in translation tools help you understand and be understood smoothly.
